Alert loved ones when a seizure occurs.

WellWear's detection system minimizes medical response time
to epileptic seizures, saving lives and money.

Save lives

By combining multiple sources of biometric data, we are able to create a highly accurate picture of seizure activity. Our cutting-edge notification system, powered by the data we collect, ensures the safety of individuals with epilepsy by summoning help when they need it most.

Save money

On average, health insurance companies pay around $15,000 in medical expenditures for each emergency room visit linked to a breakthrough seizure. Our rapid-detection technology decreases the likelihood that a seizure goes unnoticed, driving down costs incurred through unintended self-harm.

Be well

WellWear provides epileptic patients with a greater peace of mind that they will be cared for in their moments of need, destigmatizing their condition and empowering them to lead their lives with increased autonomy.
